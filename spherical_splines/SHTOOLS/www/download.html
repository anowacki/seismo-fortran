Download

To download SHTOOLS, click on one of the following files:

SHTOOLS2.6.zip - Unix zipped directory (5 Mb)

To uncompress this file, type

unzip SHTOOLS2.6.zip

SHTOOLS2.6.tar.Z - Unix tarred and compressed directory (5 Mb)

To uncompress this file, type

uncompress SHTOOLS2.6.tar.Z
tar -xf SHTOOLS2.6.tar
