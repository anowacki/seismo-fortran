SHConfidence

       SHConfidence -  Compute the probability that two sets of spherical
       harmonic coefficients are correlated at a given degree and for a given
       correlation coefficient.

SYNOPSIS

       REAL*8 FUNCTION SHConfidence ( L, CORR )

           INTEGER   L

           REAL*8    CORR

DESCRIPTION

       SHConfidence will calculate the probability (between 0 and 1) that two
       sets of spherical harmonic coefficients are linearly correlated at a
       given degree and for a given correlation coefficient at that degree.
       This is calculated using equation A7 from Pauer et al. (2006).

ARGUMENTS

       L         (input) INTEGER

                 The spherical harmonic degree to consider.

       CORR      (input) REAL*8

                 The correlation coefficient of the two data sets at degree L.
