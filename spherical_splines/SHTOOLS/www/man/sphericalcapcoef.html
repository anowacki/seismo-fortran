SphericalCapCoef

       SphericalCapCoef - Calculate the spherical harmonic coefficients of a
                          spherical cap.

SYNOPSIS

       SUBROUTINE SphericalCapCoef ( COEF, THETA, LMAX )

           REAL*8              COEF(*), THETA

           INTEGER, OPTIONAL   LMAX

DESCRIPTION

       SphericalCapCoef will calculate the spherical harmonic coefficients of
       a spherical cap centered over the north pole. The zonal coefficients,
       returned in the array COEF, are normalized such that the degree-0 term
       is 1, and are to be used with either the geodesy 4-pi normalized or
       orthonormalized spherical harmonics.

ARGUMENTS

       COEF      (output) REAL*8, DIMENSION(*)

                 The zonal spherical harmonic coefficients of a spherical cap
                 centered over the north pole. The coefficients are calculated
                 up to spherical harmonic degree LMAX, if present, or the
                 dimension of the input array - 1. The coefficients are to be
                 used with either the geodesy 4-pi normalized or
                 orthonormalized spherical harmonics

       THETA     (input) REAL*8

                 The angular radius of the spherical cap in RADIANS.

       LMAX      (input) INTEGER, OPTIONAL

                 The maximum spherical harmonic degree to calculate the
                 spherical harmonic coefficients.
